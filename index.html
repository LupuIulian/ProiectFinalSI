    Why Choose Us

    Our Strength

    • Hygienic Food

      Our restaurant’s reputation rests squarely on the shoulders of the food we serve, both in terms of its taste and overall quality. Finding fresh sources for our ingredients will guarantee that the food you serve is of the finest possible quality and flavor. This, in turn, will entice new clients and encourage existing ones to keep in our restaurant.

    • Fresh Environment

      Our restaurant have a sustainable management systems and materials and a food selection based on products that respect animal welfare and sustainable agriculture.

    • Skilled Chefs

      Inspired by natural flavours and the turning of the seasons, Chef Koh seeks to uncover and rediscover tastes within the natural blooms of the flower – paying attention to detail, and making use of every petal. Creative, inventive and original – each item on the menu is personally designed and dreamed to life by Chef Koh, whispering reality into even the most ethereal of fantasies.
